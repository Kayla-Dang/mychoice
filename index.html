It's my choice and no one else's

My Choice exists to advocate for the right to legal and safe abortions for all women across America. Regardless of age, language, or location, we will uplift voices that fight for women's health and access to abortions. By providing accessible information and resources about abortions, we will help women protect their rights and judge how policies affect their freedom, body, and future.
